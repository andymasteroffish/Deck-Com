New Cards

Terror

Blood Ritual - 0 actions
take 1 damage, draw a card and gain an action

Mind Theft
Look at an opponent’s hand. You may select a card form it. If you do permanently add it to your deck and destroy this card

Mind Control
Target unit gains an “Attack Friend” card

Attack Friend
Attack card that can only target allies
No other attack card may be played while this is in hand. This cannot be discarded
